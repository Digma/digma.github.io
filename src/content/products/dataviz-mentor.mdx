DataViz Mentor is a GPT to help you make better data visualizations. It now available for free to all ChatGPT Plus subscribers [here](https://chat.openai.com/g/g-rk1495Cu2-dataviz-mentor).

## DataViz Mentor

Creating quality data visualizations that tell a story is hard. DataViz Mentor simplifies this process. It acts as an assistant and provide feedback on your charts or visualizations. 
It guides you with recommendations on how to improve the provided chart or plot.

To use it, simply:

1. Upload an image of your plot

Then, you will directly get personalized feedback and recommendation on how you could improve it.
